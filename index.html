About Us

Welcome to Owlang Tutors!
Our company headquarters is located in Delaware, with teachers all over the world. Owlang Tutors offers fully immersive, one-on-one, and group online language courses provided by highly qualified tutors. Our curriculum uses a flipped-classroom model to build critical thinking skills and awaken creativity. The goal is to break education barriers. Our tutors are a key part of accomplishing this goal. The connections established with students foster a positive learning experience and develop a genuine love for the language. The founders of our company have many years of experience as educators and managers of people. Owlang Tutors is based on their experience and passion for education and engaging teenagers and adult learners. We believe together we can make a difference. You don’t have to be alone in your mission. There are many just like you. We feel your pain and we are here to help you gain that necessary knowledge to feel more comfortable.
